Welcome

Hello, and welcome to the ladder league home page! Here you will find scores and standings for Gregg's House Table Tennis Ladder League. To get started, just click on a name on the left. Once there, you can click on individual matches to see their outcomes.

What is this?

Primarily, this project has been an excuse to familiarize myself with Django.
It's also intended to foster competition around the table tennis table. For now, you can just view historical data, but I'm working on implementing a "challenge" mechanism to allow anyone to throw down the gauntlet from wherever you happen to be.
